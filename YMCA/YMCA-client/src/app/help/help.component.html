Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to login?

In order to sign up for a program you must login so that we can add you to the list for the program you want to sign up for. If you do not have a login you can register for free by clicking register on the login page.

Help, I forgot my password.

If you forgot your password please contact a staff member at our office

How do I signup for a program

Once you login, you will see a list of available programs you may sign up for. You can search them by name or scroll through the list. Once you find one you like you can click "signup" and you will be brought to a confirmation page. Fees can be paid on the first day that you attend

How do I see what programs I signed up for?

If you signed up for a program, you will see a blue button on the top of the program listings to see the programs you signed up for. On this screen you can also cancel programs as well

If I am a staff member how do I create a program?

Staff members can see a list of all programs and are able to create them by clicking "Create New Program" on the program listing page.

If I am a staff how do I manage users?

As a staff member, there will be a new page that allows user management, follow the instructions on the page
